## 1. 本轮目标

目标是获得：

- V1 的自然成图质量。
- V2 的线条克制。
- 外轮廓保持粗深。
- 内部分缝线使用“该区域机身色加深 20-30%”的同色系线，不用黑线/深褐线。
- 分缝数量对齐 V2 的克制水平。
- 无铆钉、通风口等微细节。

- 工具：内置 `image_gen`
- 模式：同一 prompt 从零生成 3 次。
- 未使用 V1/V2/V3 成品图作为编辑目标。
- 未做任何线条后处理；仅生成 35% 等比缩小预览条和并排图。
- C盘生成缓存保留，F盘项目目录为本轮交付路径。

## 4. Prompt 变更说明

以 V1 完整 prompt 为基准，只新增一行 `Line treatment`。其余 V1 描述保持不动。

新增行：

```text
Line treatment: keep the outer silhouette contour as a thick deep clean outline; internal seam lines must be same-hue lines made by darkening the nearby hull color by only 20-30%, never black or dark brown. Keep the number of internal seams restrained at the V2 level: only broad main hull separation seams, no rivets, no small ventilation ports, no tiny micro-detail strokes.
```

## 5. 完整 Prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: game art direction sheet for a WeChat mini-game, candidate image only
Primary request: one wide landscape concept sheet showing exactly five cute light-sci-fi starships arranged in a clean horizontal row, one ship for each class: assault, guard, artillery, support, engineering.
Scene/backdrop: pure light warm off-white background, no scene, no space, no UI, no text.
Subject and layout: five separate starships, evenly spaced left to right, same visual scale, 3/4 top-front presentation view, full silhouettes visible with generous padding. Left to right:
1) Assault ship: slender arrow / dart silhouette, fast forward wedge, rounded nose, red-orange accent.
2) Guard ship: broad thick shield silhouette, sturdy front guard, rounded armor mass, blue-teal accent.
3) Artillery ship: long elegant heavy-cannon silhouette, one integrated central cannon barrel, strong recoil fins, yellow-red accent.
4) Support ship: rounder body with soft ring wings / halo-like side wings, gentle medical/support energy pods, mint-white accent.
5) Engineering ship: small carrier/mothership silhouette with two tiny docked utility drones as part of the design, green-orange accent.
Style/medium: high-definition clean cel-shaded cartoon game concept art, bright warm Q-cute light sci-fi, large simple color planes, bold clean outline, glossy highlights, one-piece molded hull feeling similar to a single smooth cartoon aircraft shell.
Materials/textures: smooth enamel-like metal, simple big surfaces, very limited seams, no busy mechanical texture.
Composition/framing: horizontal lineup sheet, clear silhouette differentiation, each ship readable at mobile-game size.
Line treatment: keep the outer silhouette contour as a thick deep clean outline; internal seam lines must be same-hue lines made by darkening the nearby hull color by only 20-30%, never black or dark brown. Keep the number of internal seams restrained at the V2 level: only broad main hull separation seams, no rivets, no small ventilation ports, no tiny micro-detail strokes.
Constraints: pure starships only, no pilots, no characters, no faces, no eyes, no mouths, no animals, no weapons floating outside the hull, no scene props. Each ship must feel like one integrated solid shell with at most three broad structural separation seams. Use big color blocks, clean thick contour lines, and a few highlights.
Avoid: LEGO, toy bricks, mosaic, pixel blocks, grid cells, square tile assembly, panel lines, dense greebles, vents, grilles, honeycomb texture, circuit patterns, bolts everywhere, war-machine realism, Gundam/mecha look, dark cyberpunk, black space background, gritty texture, painterly brush grain, paper texture, low resolution, watermark, logo, any readable text.
```
